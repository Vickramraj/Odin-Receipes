Gulab Jamun Recipe | How to make Soft Gulab Mamun with Milk Powder in a detailed photo and video recipe. Perhaps one of the most popular Indian sweet recipes across India. It is one such dessert that is not only liked by all age groups but can also be attempted by all age groups. Traditionally the Gulab jamoon is made with store-bought pre-mix, but the same can be made by combining milk powder, plain flour, and baking powder.


Ingredients

For Jamun

  • ¾ cup (100 grams) milk powder, unsweetened
  • ½ cup (60 grams) maida / plain flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • milk, for kneading
  • ghee or oil, for frying

For Sugar Syrup

  • 2 cup sugar
  • 2 cup water
  • 2 cardamom
  • ¼ tsp saffron / kesar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp rose water

Steps

Making Sugar Syrup

  1. Boil sugar and water in a sauce pan till it gets sticky./li>
  2. Usual ratio for sugar to water is 1 cup of sugar to 2 cups of water and the boiling time is around 5 to 10 mins to get sticky texture.

Making Gulab Jamun

  1. You will be needing khoya, milk powder, flour, sooji and the measurements are adjusted
  2. Make sure the gulab jamun dough is soft and smooth.
  3. Take small portion of dough and shape it into a smooth ball without giving any pressure.
  4. You have to fry gulab jamuns low and slow till it gets golden in colour.
  5. Your jamuns and syrup has to be hot or warm when you soak them. This way they absorb the syrup better.
  6. Serve gulab jamuns hot, warm or cold. You can serve them with vanilla ice cream.
